A space for understanding, support, and connection.

Why join the conversation?

We encourage you to participate actively, share your thoughts, and connect with others who understand the unique challenges you face. This is your space to be heard, find support, and contribute to a community of straight white men navigating a changing world together.

It's crucial to ensure that comments on this website cannot be perceived as discriminatory or hateful. The focus should be on providing support and understanding for straight white men finding it difficult to be themselves in our ever changing social environment.

Dos and don'ts

Where's what you can expect:

  • Free speech
    Express your opinions and perspectives without fear of being shadow banned or de-platformed.
  • Moderated discussions
    We maintain a respectful environment by ensuring discussions remain constructive and free from personal attacks or hate speech.
  • Meaningful connections
    Engage in thoughtful conversations with others who understand your experiences.
  • Community support
    Find support, share advice, and build camaraderie with like-minded individuals.

Ground rules:

  • Respectful dialogue
    Treat everyone with courtesy and respect, even when disagreeing.
  • Moderated discussions
    Bigotry, discrimination, and personal attacks will not be tolerated.
  • Constructive criticism
    Focus on ideas and issues, not personal attacks.
  • Confidentiality
    Respect the privacy of others and do not share personal information outside the forum.

Your privacy matters

We understand that discussing sensitive topics requires a safe and secure environment. Rest assured that anything you post here is confidential and will not be shared with any third-party organisations. Your privacy is our priority.
