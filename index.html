You're not alone

The world is changing rapidly, and sometimes it feels like the rules of the game have been rewritten overnight. If you're a straight white man, you might be feeling marginalised, misunderstood, or even blamed for things you don't feel responsible for. We are here to help!

We feel the same!

With rapid social changes, it's easy to feel like the ground is constantly shifting beneath your feet. Are you a straight white male over 50 who feels like your voice is being silenced? Do you feel like your opinions and experiences are dismissed or labeled as "privileged," even when your reality feels far from it? Perhaps you're concerned that merit and hard work are becoming less important than meeting quotas, or you worry about saying the wrong thing and facing unintended consequences.

If any of this resonates, you're not alone. This website is a resource for those who feel like they're navigating a minefield of constantly evolving social norms and expectations. We're here to provide a safe space for open dialogue, offer practical tools for navigating challenging conversations, and help you find answers that empower you to thrive in the modern world.

Safety in numbers!

Our goal is to create a supportive community where individuals feel heard, respected, and empowered to thrive in the modern workplace. Working together we can survive this mess!
